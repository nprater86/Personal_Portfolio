About Me

Background

I was born and raised in Michigan (near the Port Huron area). I graduated from Yale High School in Yale, MI in 2005. I then attended the University of Michgain, Ann Arbor and graduated in 2010 with a Bachelor of Science degree. After college, I moved to Chicago and worked at CVS Pharmacy as a pharmacy technician until February 2012, when I got a job at OptumRx in account management, where I worked for just under 10 years. At the end of 2021, I decided to quit my job and attend Coding Dojo to pursue a career in web development.

I am currently still living in Chicago in North Center along with my beautiful wife, Aly, my two sons, Chase (4) and Ari (3 months), and my poodle mutt Rosy (7). I love living in the city and don't ever plan on leaving!
